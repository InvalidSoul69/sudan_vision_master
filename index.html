Vision

Reaching comprehensive community development and building a strong, cohesive society.

Mission

We and our qualified staff work in cooperation with our partners, to ensure that every individual in our society lives a dignified life free of oppression, discrimination and injustice, to eliminate poverty and hunger and to support innovation and communities to shape their future to satisfy their ambitions.

Our Values

  • Integrity and non-profitability
  • Voluntarism
  • Accountability
  • Transparency
  • Cooperation beyond borders
  • Respect for human rights
  • Responsibility and Accountability

Our Approach

Sudanese Vision It takes an integrated approach that focuses on empowering communities by sponsoring community-led initiatives and building the capabilities of community members to provide humanitarian services that alleviate suffering, enhance human security, eliminate poverty and hunger, improve health, and enhance the community’s ability to manage, operate and maintain these services.

About Us

Sudanese Vision for Development Organization (SVDO) is a National Sudanese NGO established and Registered in (2020) number (598) under the act of Humanitarian aid Commission (HAC) is We are a non-political Organization that aims to promote the principles of social justice, Human rights and the rule of law, to facilitate access to a comprehensive societal development and support the peace and development process. We firmly believe that dignity is an inherent right for every human being, on the basis that he lives a life free of oppression, discrimination and injustice.

Therefore, we seek to empower communities and protect groups. vulnerable people, defending human rights and establishing social justice by offering and providing comprehensive humanitarian services through many integrated initiatives and interventions, which include providing urgent support to communities affected by disasters in times of need.

Our team consists of individuals passionate about humanitarian work who are dedicated to championing human rights and social Development by working closely with local communities, civil society Organizations, government institutions, and local and international NGOs, to catalyze social and legal reforms that protect the rights of all Sudanese citizens to build a strong and cohesive society

our partners

We believe that working with partner organizations produces large and sustainable projects to serve target groups. Mobilizing expertise and resources enables us to learn, maximize our impact, and quickly reach support for those in need. We are constantly searching for new partners who share our values and devote their efforts to making a positive change in the lives of others

Our Objectives

Improving the level of Education: enhancing the quality of education and increasing access to it for all segments of society.

Health Care Development: Improving the quality of health care and providing it to all, including preventive and curative campaigns.

Raise the legal awareness and widen the scope of knowledge regarding legal affairs

Combating Poverty and Unemployment and working to achieve a fair distribution of resources and services.

Working on Promoting of human rights and Social Justice for all


Promoting and Protecting Human Rights and Equality among all individuals

Environmental protection: Supporting initiatives and projects aimed at protecting the environment and preserving natural resources.

Strengthening national culture and identity: Preserving cultural heritage and enhancing national identity.
